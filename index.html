Overview

The Entrepreneurship center is setup with the goal to create an Entrepreneurial ecosystem in the campus and foster Entrepreneurship among students. We will help a potential entrepreneur

  • Identify a business opportunity.
  • Identify the purpose of the business.
  • Finalize a business opportunity.
  • Access Intellectual Property Rights Consultants.
  • Access Chartered Accountants and Advocates.

Through our center we will provide a platform to develop ideas, build teams and exchange experience. We will provide an exposure to the entrepreneurial world through seminars from successful entrepreneurs and providing internships in a scaling up entrepreneurial venture. The main goal of the club is to inspire students to choose entrepreneurship as a career and provide them with all the necessary tools to make them a competitive entrepreneur.

Objectives

  • Create an entrepreneurial ecosystem in the campus.
  • Support SME's(Small and Medium Enterprises) to face the challenge of survival and growth.
  • Support Family Business to face internal and exernal problems.
  • Over a period ACE and FB must evolve into a self sustaining entity.
